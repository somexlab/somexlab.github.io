We are delighted to welcome [Zhibo Li]({{< relref "/authors/Zhibo/_index.md" >}}) to our research group at the University of Vienna! Zhibo brings expertise in microfluidics and active matter, along with a strong enthusiasm for pushing the boundaries of soft matter physics.

In his new role, he will investigate the complex mechanical behavior of tissues at the mesoscale and study cellular dynamics in biomimetic emulsions.

We look forward to collaborating, learning from one another, and achieving exciting scientific advances together. Welcome aboard, Zhibo!
