[Roberto]({{< relref "/authors/admin/_index.md" >}}) recently joined the growing community of physicists, chemists, and engineers dedicated to advancing the science and technology of particle characterisation, serving as a committee member of the [Particle Characterisation Interest Group](https://pcig.co.uk/). The group's goals are:

- Advance particle characterisation by promoting cutting-edge methods across science and industry.

- Foster collaboration and knowledge exchange among researchers, scientists, and industry professionals.

- Support methodological and technological innovation in particle characterisation techniques.

- Promote standards and best practices to ensure accuracy, reliability, and reproducibility.
